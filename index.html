This application helps you to process your pass photo so that you can easily adjust your photo according to pass requirements,
The produced pass photo have exact required dimensions and format.
Please read the instruction on the police pages!

  • Drop or select your pass photo.
  • Move and resize the photo either with mouse or buttons below
  • Press the accept button
  • Right click on the image above to save your ready made passphoto.
Now, you can upload your passphoto at the police pass application page.
